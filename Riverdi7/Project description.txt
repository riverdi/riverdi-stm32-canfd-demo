Phase 1: Battery Simulator:
At this phase, the 7inch shall act as BMS of the battery and start charging/discharging the battery and display the charging process.
Also, during the charging, the user LED shall blink each 200ms to indicate as charging process near the charging socket of the EV.
--------------------------Done----------------------------------

Phase 2: Battery Level and Status Transmission over CAN-FD Bus.
The CAN-FD Driver has been developed and able to send the battery status and level to Riverdi 10.1" over the CAN-FD Bus.
Also, the LED will blink when the simulation is charging the battery.

Connection:
Please connect CAN1 of Riverdi 7.0" to CAN1 of Riverdi 10.1".
